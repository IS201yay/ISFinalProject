Fun Facts About Dolphins

  • Dolphins have 2 stomachs
  • Dolphins dive up to 1,000 feet
  • Dolphins can live up to 50 years
  • Dolphins never chew their food
  • Dolphins can swim at 20 miles per hour

Different Types of Dolphins

  1. Bottle Nose dolphin
  2. Striped dolphin
  3. Spinner dolphin
  4. Baiji dolphin
  5. Amazon River dolphin

Dolphins are itelligent and magestic creatures that can be found in every ocean, and are at least 8 million in population size.
